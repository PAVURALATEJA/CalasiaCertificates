Sync calibration PDFs from Dropbox into the database automatically.

How Dropbox Sync Works
① Calibration Manager
Uploads PDF to Dropbox folder /Calibration_Certificates/
② Naming Rule
File must be named SERIAL_CERTNUM.pdf
e.g. FLK87V-001_CAL2025-001.pdf
③ Auto or Manual Sync
System auto-syncs every 10 minutes, or click "Sync Now"
④ Customer Sees It
Certificate appears in customer dashboard immediately after sync
